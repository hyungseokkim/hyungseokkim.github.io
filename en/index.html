About Me

I am a web backend developer with 7 years of professional experience, primarily focused on analyzing and improving existing legacy systems rather than building greenfield features from scratch. My core expertise lies in backend development using Java (Spring Boot) and PHP, with extensive hands-on experience in data modeling, migration, and integrity validation using major RDBMS such as MySQL and PostgreSQL.
